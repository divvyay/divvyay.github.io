Walking Tour Map

I was tasked with creating a walking tour set in Madison with a unique topic that both visitors and residents may not be aware of. As a student who gets most of their work done outside of their place of residence, I decided to create a tour of my favorite, lesser-known study spots in and around UW-Madison campus. I used geojson.io to create my route.
