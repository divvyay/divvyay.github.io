Monsoon and Malaria

I used ArcGIS Story Maps to create a Scrollytelling map displaying and describing the connection between monsoon season and malaria cases in India. Monsoon rains produce conditions for mosquito breeding, leading to a spike in malaria cases country-wide. By looking at regions with heavy rainfall and combining it with malaria case data, the map reveals patterns in the timing and severity of outbreaks.
