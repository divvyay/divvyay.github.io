hey! i'm divya.

growing up artsy, around tech, and as a lifelong geography nerd, i naturally ended up at the intersection of all three.

i’m a proud multipotentialite who, while studying a wide variety of subjects, has always seen them as ones that build off each other. being able to approach my work from multiple perspectives is something i pride myself in.
